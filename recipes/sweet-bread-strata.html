Sweet Bread Strata

Home

Description

A simple strata made with Hawaiian sweet bread. You can easily alter the fillings: substitute sausage for the bacon, use different cheeses, add some mushrooms, etc. Let your imagination be your guide!

Ingredients

  • 6 Hawaiian bread rolls (such as King's®)
  • ½ cup shredded Swiss cheese, divided
  • ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese, divided
  • 3 slices cooked bacon, diced, divided
  • 4 green onions, diced, divided
  • 3 eggs
  • ⅔ cup half-and-half
  • ½ cup skim milk
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cut each roll into 8 cubes.
  3. Spread onto a baking sheet in a single layer. Bake in the preheated oven until toasted, about 10 minutes.
  4. Layer toasted bread cubes in a 1 1/2-quart baking pan. Sprinkle 1/4 cup Swiss cheese, 1/4 cup Cheddar cheese, 1/2 the bacon, and 2 green onions on top of the bread. Shake to settle the toppings into the bread.
  5. Beat eggs together with half-and-half, milk, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Pour over the bread cubes, pressing cubes down into the mixture.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining Swiss cheese, Cheddar cheese, bacon, green onions, and Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until strata is hot in the center, about 30 minutes.
