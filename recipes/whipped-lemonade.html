Whipped Lemonade

Home

Description

This 3-ingredient whipped lemonade, made with freshly-squeezed lemon juice, cream, and sweetened condensed milk, blends into a frothy, summery, creamy lemonade drink in seconds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 cups ice

Steps

  1. Combine heavy cream, lemon juice, sweetened condensed milk, and ice in a blender cup. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
